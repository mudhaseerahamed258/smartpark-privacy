Child Safety Standards

Protecting the youngest members of our community. Official guidelines for SmartParkConnect residential areas.

🚸

Designated Safe Zones

Children should only play in designated play areas, parks, and clubhouses. Under no circumstances are the basement parking lots or driving lanes to be used as recreational areas.

🚗

Strict Speed Limits

All vehicles must adhere to a strict 10 km/h (6 mph) speed limit within the community gates, specially near entry/exit ramps, blind corners, and designated pedestrian crossings.

📱

Geofence Alerts via App

SmartParkConnect employs geofencing technology. Parents can opt-in to receive instant notifications if their child’s registered tag or device wanders too close to high-traffic vehicle gates.

👁️

AI-Assisted Surveillance

Our parking camera systems are equipped with AI behavior detection. If a young child is spotted unaccompanied in the basement parking, security is immediately alerted to safely escort them back.

🛑

Visitor Awareness

When you generate a Visitor Pass via SmartParkConnect, guests are automatically informed of our active Child Safety Protocols on their digital pass. All visitors must comply.

📞

Emergency Protocols

In the event of an emergency, the SmartParkConnect app features a one-tap SOS button that instantly connects you to community security, medical personnel, and estate management.

Parental Responsibilities & Guidelines

While SmartParkConnect technology significantly boosts community safety, parental supervision remains the most critical layer of protection.

  • Ensure children under 10 are supervised by an adult or responsible guardian at all times in common areas.
  • Teach children to use pedestrian walkways and never to stand near automated parking barriers.
  • Keep your contact information updated in the SmartParkConnect app to ensure urgent alerts reach you.
  • Report any reckless driving or safety non-compliance anonymously via the app's Community portal.
