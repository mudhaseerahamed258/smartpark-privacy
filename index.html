Privacy Policy - SmartPark Connect

Last updated: April 2026

SmartPark Connect ("we", "our", "app") provides smart vehicle and parking management services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email
  • Vehicle details (number plate, type)
  • Organization details (for community parking)
  • Location data (for parking & vehicle search)

2. How We Use Data

  • Vehicle identification and search
  • Visitor parking management
  • Organization-based access control
  • Emergency contact features

3. Data Security

  • Secure APIs (HTTPS)
  • Authentication and authorization
  • Database protection

4. User Rights

  • Update profile
  • Delete account
  • Request data removal

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication)
  • Google Maps (location)
